A Note on Timing

Because Aesthetics Has a Timeline

Some treatments take time to deliver full results. Botox settles in 10–14 days. Laser treatments often require a series of sessions spaced weeks apart. Fillers integrate over several weeks. Surgical recovery has its own timeline.

This means the best time to start your aesthetic journey is almost always before you need the results — not the week before your reunion, wedding, or gala.
